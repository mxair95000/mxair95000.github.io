GonRonGo：Designing Suitable Carpool Services for Rural Areas

2021-03-29
Challenge

How to provide affordable and sustainable public transportation services to Wanrong Township in Hualien, a sparsely populated rural area with insufficient public transportation?

Solution

The "GonRonGO"（共榮GO） carpool service was designed to use the local early retirement population to transport students and older adults. The service is designed to encourage students and patients to ride on the same bus to improve efficiency and reduce costs.

Contribution

UI/UX Design、Prototyping、Field Research

Design Team

蔡宜謹、陳熙、許馨毅、丁俊傑、黃嘉旭、黃佳祺

Time

2018.06 - 2018.12

Award

🏅 2018 DIGI+ Talent Honorable Mention Award












Background



Wanrong(Malibasi, 萬榮鄉) is a rural township which is located southeast of Hualien, Taiwan. With a population of about 6,500～ 7,000, 93.12% of residents are Malibasi people. Ministry of Transportation and Communications (MOTC) introduced the DRTS (Demand Responsive Transit System) at the end of 2017, hoping to solve the commuting needs. However, according to the report, the usage rate is quite a law, was less than 20 People Per Month, which shows DRTS doesn’t meet residents’ needs. To understand the problems faced by DRTS, the team took a four-day field survey in Wanrong to design a new transportation service.




Research



In order to gain a deeper understanding of the problems faced by DRTS in the local context, we conduct field research to understand local needs and design solutions.During the four days of study, we interviewed 32 local people, including Civil servants, students and older people, among six different villages and tribes.

We found that residents Seldom use public transit because of the poor on-time performance and limited routes. With the budget shortage, MOTC’s DRTS can’t be “Demand Responsive”, and hard to meet different users’ needs. Most local people never use the DRTS. Older Adults with Chronic ill have to spend lots of money on TAXI for monthly follow-up appointments. Because the bus stop is far from the tribe, only the taxi can offer door-to-door transportation.




Changelle and Opportunity


Due to the Sparsely populated environment and unreliable public transit, students have to wake up more early for school, and older adults need to spend more money to go to the hospital. On the other side, we found local area has a strong community relationship and a high ownership rate of cars and smartphones. It shows carpool has the potential to solve the transportation problem.






Solution:

GonRonGo 共榮Go — A Commuting Carpool Service App


To focus on commuting needs and meet the carpool regulations, users could only carpool twice a day, one for left and another one for return. Passengers have to order a carpool before 19:00. From 19:00 to 20:00 system will send carpool requests to the suitable riders, and the rider could choose to accept. At 21:00, everyone will know their matching result.




Meet the Needs of Local People



Persona concludes there are kinds of users: Ali, the student; Jiru, a chronic disease older adult; and Bayan, the office worker. The residents like Bayan often ride downtown, where are the hospital and school located. So Bayan could be the carpool rider to pick up tribe residents like Jiru and Ali to the destination. Rider's gas fee could pay with MoTC's allowance for DRTS.

In that case, everyone could get what they need, and the government’s DRTS budget could be used more effectiveness




Register and Real-name Authentication



Register account must scan the Taiwan ID/NHI Card for Real-name authentication to ensure every user’s safety. Only rider users need to upload driver’s licenses. After manual review, the user could start to use GonRonGo.




Send the Carpool Requset



Eight hours before departure, both user and rider could send a carpool request. Users can invite friends from the same school or hospital to share a ride through the “Carpool Partner” function. After sending the request, the system will send the request to the suitable user.




Arrived Destination with GonRonGo！




On the day of departure, the app will display the task information of the day and remind the user to departure. The rider screen APP will show the boarding point of each ride-sharing passenger and the estimated time of arrival at the destination. If the passenger does not show up, the rider clicks the button to report the issue. Too many no-shows will make the account deactivated for seven days.




Impact: Honour and Award



At the end of the internship period, our app “GonRonGo 共榮Go— Carpooling Service for the rural area” won the Ministry of Economic Affairs’ 2018 DIGI+ Talent Award. We believe this pilot study could encourage government agencies to use carpool as a more flexible and affordable solution for rural areas transportation.




Reflection: Challenges to Rural Area



After six months of user research and design, we create GonRonGo（共榮GO） App, a carpool app match local people to get carpool. Still, To sovle problem in rural area, a app is far from enough.There are a lot of problem need to be solve. First, during the field research , we found there are many of address of rural area doesn’t include in the Google Map. which affects daily life and tourism development. Second, the exsiting laws of carpool are not flexible, which will making process of design new transportation service become slow. Third, serious rural depopulation affect local people’s life, lots of older adults can’t get enough care from family.To make a well-being community, we still need interdisciplinary expert to improve rural area continuily.
